B.E. Admission Guidelines & Registration

Eligibility Criteria (B.E. Degree)

  • Candidate must be an Indian National.
  • Must have passed HSC (12th Standard) examination of Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education or its equivalent examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.
  • Obtained at least 45% marks (at least 40% marks in case of candidates of Backward class categories, Economically Weaker Section and Persons with Disability belonging to Maharashtra State only) in compulsory subjects added together.
  • Must have obtained a non-zero positive score in MHT-CET 2026 or JEE (Main) 2026 examination.
  • For Direct Second Year B.E. Admissions: Diploma in Engineering and Technology with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories).

Selection Process

1

CAP Online Registration

Register online on the State Common Entrance Test Cell (CET Cell) Maharashtra portal and fill out the centralized application form.

2

Document Verification

Visit the designated Facilitation Center (FC) for verification of original documents and confirmation of application form.

3

Option Form & CAP Allotment

Fill out online option form specifying college codes. Seats are allotted through DTE CAP rounds based on merit scores.

4

Reporting & Admission

Report to allotted college with original documents and fees to confirm B.E. admission seat.
