B.E. Degree Programs

Academic Courses & Portals

B.E. in Computer Engineering

Comprehensive study of algorithms, databases, OS, full-stack programming, cloud architecture, and artificial intelligence models.

Intake: 120 Seats Duration: 4 Years
B.E. in Information Technology

Focuses on full-stack web architectures, DevOps tools, secure software design, data forensics, and cloud application deployments.

Intake: 60 Seats Duration: 4 Years
B.E. in Mechanical Engineering

Focuses on fluid engineering, thermal systems, manufacturing shops, CAD/CAM designing, and industrial robotics modeling.

Intake: 60 Seats Duration: 4 Years
B.E. in Electrical Engineering

Emphasizes power system configurations, high voltage distributions, smart grid systems, electric machine designs, and EV controls.

Intake: 60 Seats Duration: 4 Years
B.E. in ENTC Engineering

Delivers skills in embedded circuits, VLSI, microcontrollers, digital signal processing (DSP), and 5G cellular communication.

Intake: 60 Seats Duration: 4 Years
